Diabetes Predictor

Diabetes Risk Prediction,
Report Generator

This web-based application leverages machine learning to predict the likelihood of diabetes based on user-provided health metrics. The tool offers a quick and simple way to assess diabetes risk by entering information such as glucose levels, BMI, age, and more. The application provides personalized recommendations to manage and reduce the risk of diabetes and generates a downloadable PDF report summarizing the results. The goal of this project is to promote proactive health management through early detection of potential risks.

Parameters Used in Diabetes Prediction

This application uses several health parameters to predict the likelihood of diabetes. Below are the key parameters, along with their significance and formulas where applicable:

  • Insulin (µU/mL): Insulin levels in the blood.
  • Glucose (mg/dL): Plasma glucose concentration measured 2 hours after a glucose tolerance test.
  • Body Mass Index (BMI): Calculated using the formula:
    BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]²
  • Blood Pressure (mmHg): Diastolic blood pressure.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A measure that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history.
  • Skin Thickness (mm): Triceps skinfold thickness.
